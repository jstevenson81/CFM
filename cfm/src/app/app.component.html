Getting Started

So you're ready to go? The steps below will help you on your journey.

  • Make The Choice

    Call, email or come on by!

    Call 985.951.2255, email info@cfmandeville.com or or stop by to speak with one of our experienced staff to find out more about our programs and set up an appointment for our Fundamentals Program if you are new to CrossFit.

  • Fundamentals (REQUIRED)

    Dive into perfect movement!

    Fundamentals – A 2 session course that will be taught in a small group setting with a coach introducing you to our 9 foundational movements that are used in class. Before entering the general CrossFit classes, we will take this time to get you familiar with how a class is run, what a WOD is, and find out more about YOU!

    The course fee is $100 which includes the 2 sessions and a CFM shaker bottle.

  • You're Ready!

    Crossfit Classes

    You're now ready to dive into becoming the best you! You will have access to CrossFit classes on the calendar once your 2 Fundamentals sessions have been completed.

Coaches

Brian Oatsvall, Coach and Owner

Certifications: Level 1

Brian is from Mobile, AL and has lived in Mandeville since 2006. He received his undergraduate and MBA from The University of Alabama. Brian discovered CrossFit in 2010 and later obtained his Level 1 Certification in 2015. That same year he participated in the CrossFit Open and finished 68th in the word in his age division. Brian has valued fitness his entire life and is a firm believer that fitness can be attained no matter the age. Brian’s favorite part of CrossFit is working out with his fiancé, Brittany. Brian currently coaches the Comp Train Masters class.

Marcus Speed, Coach and Partner

Certifications: Level 1, Strongman

Marcus, a northshore native, is an Level 1 trainer. He became hooked on Crossfit in 2013, and received his L1 in 2014. Getting fit and hanging out with like-minded people...What's better?!”
