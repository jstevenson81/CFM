Ryan Member Spotlight for June, 2018

  • NAME, AGE, OCCUPATION:
    Ryan, 23, Software Developer
  • How long have you been doing Crossfit and what class do you normally attend?
    About 9 months. 4:30 class.
  • What made you start doing Crossfit?
    My buddy kept pestering me to improve my lifestyle and he believed that CrossFit was the perfect way to do that. Eventually I ran out of excuses to not try it.
  • What is your favorite lift or movement?
    Definitely back squats.
  • What do you enjoy most about Crossfit?
    The thing that I enjoy most about CrossFit is the class like structure.
  • Tell us a fun fact about yourself?
    The muscles in my eyes are different than normal so I can somewhat individually control where each eye is looking.
  • What is your athletic background?
    Some martial arts when I was younger, and a very limited experience with weight lifting.
  • How has Crossfit changed your life/lifestyle/level of fitness?
    Physically I have lost weight and generally feel healthier, but mentally CrossFit has helped with my happiness, confidence, and self-esteem.
  • What is your proudest Crossfit achievement?
    So far that would be being able to deadlift my body weight.
  • Name one goal/personal achievement that you would like to accomplish through crossfit?
    Being able to do ring muscle ups one day would be pretty cool.
  • What are your hobbies/interests outside of CF?
    Video games and occasionally shooting my bow.
  • What advice would you give to new members?
    Personally, I had a bad habit of making up excuses to not go when I first started. So the biggest tip that I can think of is to not get into the habit of talking yourself out of going. Making CrossFit a normal part of my daily routine was what helped me the most with that.
  • What is your favorite cheat meal?
    Canes all the way!
