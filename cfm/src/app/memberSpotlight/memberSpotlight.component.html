Melissa Jones member spotlight for August, 2017

  • Name: Melissa Jones Age: 49
  • Occupation: Event coordinator for Jones Physical Therapy
  • How long have you been doing CF and what class do you normally attend?
    I’ve been doing CF for 3 ½ years but consistently for a year and a half. I attend 6:00 am and Skinny Fit. I also enjoy Saturday am Team WOD. It’s fun to catch up with friends that work out at different times.
  • What made you start doing CF?
    I have always worked out and ran but was very bored with the same routine and wanted to try something new and challenging. I convinced my husband Paul to join with me since it was something we could do together. That is one of the greatest things about CF. It is like a mini date every morning. Haha
  • What is your athletic background?
    I have always enjoyed working out and been a gym member since high school. I did group cardio classes, spin, and played tennis for several years.
  • What's your favorite lift/movement
    My favorite movement is power cleans.
  • How has CF changed your life/lifestyle/level of fitness?
    I am in the best shape of my life. I am able to carry all of my groceries in one trip!
  • What do you enjoy most about CF?
    What I enjoy most about CF is the variety of movements and endless combinations of workout. I have yet to get bored! I added Skinny Fit to my program and it has really helped my endurance. I would recommend everyone give it a try.
  • What is your proudest CF achievement?
    My proudest achievements are pullups and double unders. It took a long time to get them but once it clicked it was “easy”.
  • Name one goal/personal achievement that you would like to accomplish through CF.
    I would love to be able to string together toes to bar!
  • What are your hobbies/interests outside of CF?
    Outside of CF, I like to travel, read, hang out with my teenagers and play with my 3 dogs.
  • What advice would you give to new members?
    Consistency! Show up every day even if there are movements that you haven’t mastered. You can scale them. Listen to the coaches!
  • What is your favorite cheat meal?
    PIZZA! And not the kind that is delivered to your house.
