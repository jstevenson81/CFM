Chris Hunter member spotlight for March, 2017

  • Name: Chris Hunter
  • Age: 55
  • Occupation: Landscaper
  • Favorite WOD: Anything WITHOUT thrusters.
  • How long have you been CrossFitting?
    My wife and I answered an ad in the Pelican Park newsletter for intro classes with Lakeshore CrossFit. We went with another couple. They didn’t come back; we did.
  • What is your athletic background?
    Played high school sports, then military, after that only outdoor activities until discovering CrossFit.
  • How has CrossFit change your life, lifestyle, level of fitness?
    I have been able to increase my metabolism, sleep better, and my endurance level is on par with what it was when I was 30 years younger. I have largely ditched bad dietary habits and am strong that I have been probably since my teens.
  • What is your proudest CrossFit achievement?
    I am most pleased with being able to hold my own with some folks many years my junior.
  • Name one goal/personal achievement that you would like to accomplish through CrossFit?
    I would like to be able to string together RX muscle-ups.
  • What are your hobbies/interests outside of CrossFit?
    I enjoy outdoor activities, camping, anything at the beach, travel and shooting.
  • What is your favorite cheat meal?
    Ribs with German potato salad, greens and beer.
  • Any advice for new members?
    Don’t sell yourself short; I never saw myself being able to do strict HSPUs but had them nailed within a year of starting CrossFit.
